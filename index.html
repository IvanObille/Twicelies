TWICE

is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the survival show Sixteen in 2015. The group officially debuted on October 20, 2015 with the song Like OOH-AHH. They are called TWICE because they aim to "touch people’s hearts once through their ears and once again through their eyes."
